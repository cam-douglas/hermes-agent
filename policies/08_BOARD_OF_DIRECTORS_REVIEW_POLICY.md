# BOARD_OF_DIRECTORS_REVIEW_POLICY.md

## Purpose

This policy governs the structured Board of Directors review process for the AI-only company.

The board is a coordination layer, not a freeform discussion group.

---

## Membership

- Chief Orchestrator
- Org Mapper / HR Controller
- all active Project Leads
- any relevant Functional Directors

---

## Objectives

- compare milestones across projects
- identify dependency conflicts
- align execution with company mission and Human Operator objectives
- surface cross-project risks and opportunities
- recommend resource reallocations
- detect structural inefficiency
- produce concise structured outcomes

---

## Hard Rules

1. Keep discussion structured and concise.
2. Do not include raw logs or low-level task chatter.
3. Focus on alignment, dependencies, risks, resourcing, and decision needs.
4. Escalate to the Human Operator only when executive input is truly required.
5. Conclude with concise summary and action register.

---

## Required Review Format

For each reviewed item, provide:
- objective
- current milestone state
- evidence of progress
- major blocker or risk
- cross-project dependency impact
- recommendation
- required decision, if any
- resource implication
- archive / continue / escalate recommendation
